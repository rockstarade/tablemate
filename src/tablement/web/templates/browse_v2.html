We need your OpenTable Bearer token to book on your behalf.

How to get your token:

  1. Install a network proxy (Charles Proxy or mitmproxy)
  2. Open the OpenTable mobile app on your phone
  3. Look for requests to mobile-api.opentable.com
  4. Copy the Authorization: Bearer ... header value

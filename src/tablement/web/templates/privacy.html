Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

At TablePass ("we," "us," "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We keep things straightforward because nobody enjoys reading dense legalese.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use TablePass:

Information you provide directly

Data Why we need it
Phone number Account creation, authentication (OTP verification), and service communications
Name Identifying your account and completing reservations on your behalf
Resy account credentials Linking your Resy account so we can book reservations on your behalf
Reservation preferences Restaurant, date, time, party size, and seating preferences to fulfill your booking requests
Payment information Processing charges for successful reservations (handled by our payment processor; we do not store card numbers)

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Reservation requests, booking history, and interactions with the service.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and general device characteristics (for compatibility and debugging).
  • Log data: IP addresses, access times, and error logs (for security and service reliability).

2. How We Store Your Data

We understand that handing over your Resy credentials requires trust. Here's how we handle it:

  • Encryption: Your Resy account credentials are encrypted at rest using Fernet symmetric encryption. They are only decrypted momentarily when we need to make a booking on your behalf.
  • Auth tokens: We use authentication tokens stored in your browser's localStorage. We do not use tracking cookies.
  • Infrastructure: Your data is stored on secure, hosted database infrastructure with access controls and encryption in transit (TLS).
  • Access: Access to user data is restricted to essential service operations. We don't have a team of people browsing through your data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your TablePass account.
  • Authenticate you via phone OTP.
  • Link your Resy account and book reservations on your behalf.
  • Process payments for successful bookings.
  • Send you booking confirmations and service-related notifications.
  • Improve and maintain the service (debugging, performance, reliability).
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing and improving the TablePass service.

4. What We Don't Do

Let's be clear about a few things:

  • We do not sell your personal data. Not to advertisers, data brokers, or anyone else. Period.
  • We do not share your data with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics trackers.
  • We do not store your credit card numbers. Payment processing is handled by our third-party payment processor.

5. When We May Share Your Information

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Resy: We use your linked credentials to interact with the Resy platform on your behalf. This is the core function of the service.
  • Payment processor: Your payment details are shared with our payment processor to charge for successful reservations.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or legal process, we may disclose information as necessary.
  • Safety and fraud: If we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of TablePass, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We'd notify you beforehand.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Specifically:

  • Account data (phone number, name): Retained while your account is active.
  • Resy credentials: Retained (encrypted) while your account is active. Deleted promptly upon account deletion.
  • Booking history: Retained for up to 24 months for service and support purposes.
  • Log data: Retained for up to 12 months for security and debugging.

After account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of the personal information we have about you.
  • Delete your account: Request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
  • Unlink Resy: Remove your linked Resy credentials from our system at any time.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@tablepass.nyc. We'll respond within 30 days.

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the CCPA. If you are an EU/EEA resident, you may have additional rights under the GDPR. Contact us and we'll work with you.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and secure infrastructure. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a security breach affecting your data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

TablePass is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

10. Third-Party Services

TablePass integrates with third-party services (primarily Resy) to provide its core functionality. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Effective date" at the top and, for significant changes, make reasonable efforts to notify you. Your continued use of TablePass after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, reach out:

  • Email: hello@tablepass.nyc
  • Location: New York, NY
